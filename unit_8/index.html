Homewares for the young at heart and free of spirit


Started by Alexandria Harbin in 2014, Seven Wonders stocks wheel thrown pottery pieces with a distinctive, joyful aesthetic. Alexandria has varous pieces available for sale and exhibition, falling into distinct collections.
The range includes:

  • spaBowls
  • spaMugs
  • spaCanisters
  • spaCoasters

FEATURED COLLECTION

Seeing spots

The seeing spots collection celebrates Alexandria's background growing up with a family heavily involved in breeding and exhibiting Dalmatian dogs, while still embracing her fun, quirky aesthetic.

Meet the artist

Alexandria grew up in coastal Wollongong, and brings this carefree, coastal lifestyle into the aesthetic of her pieces. She works out of a studio in Mascot, with her dog, Ruby always at hand.
